About

Codeveoyr started as a team of friends freelancers.

  • 2013

    Our Humble Beginnings

    A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away.. Ok, not so long and far to be honest! After making lot of work in industry, few friends thought that it would be great to start their own company

  • July 2014

    A Team is Born

    Understanding that distributed team can scale effectively ends with a few full cycle products. Distributed team management was foundation of newly created Codeveyor

  • October 2014

    Transition to Full Service

    First app, fully made by Codeveyor team for client shipped. Was decided to hire new people only for remote work, without having actual company office

  • November 2015

    Corporate github started

    In addition to tech twitter account, Codeveyor guys decided to support OSS initiative with official Codeveyor github account. We will provide implementations, extensions and enhancements we are using in our daily work. Now you can check our code quality 24/7 online
